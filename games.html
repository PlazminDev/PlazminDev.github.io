Games

Gone Fishing is a "horror" game released in April 2024 about a man named Rick Balinston who wants to go on a normal fishing trip, however he has dreams about what's happening right beneath his house. It's basically just a walking simulator with occasional fishing minigames, and even an endless mode that's unlocked after beating the game. Despite being pretty rough around the edges and gameplay that's not that fun, it's definitely the best game i've made so far. It mainly relies on atmosphere for feeling, sometimes it's calming, sometimes it's opressing. It's also the second monetized game i've released.
